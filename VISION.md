# VISION

## Project Goal

Build an automated venue scouting and outreach agent specifically designed for a Detroit-based psytrance selector to find and book venues for recurring psychedelic music nights — first in Detroit, then across the Midwest and beyond.

## The Problem

Detroit is the birthplace of techno. It has world-class underground venues, a deep electronic music culture, and a community that values experimental sound. But psytrance — forest psy, dark progressive, hi-tech, full-power nighttime — has almost no regular presence. The venues exist. The audience exists. The connection is missing because nobody is systematically finding the right spaces and making the ask.

This agent closes that gap.

## Core Philosophy

- **Detroit-First**: Every search query, qualification prompt, and pitch template is shaped by knowledge of the Detroit underground — Corktown warehouses, Hamtramck DIY spaces, Midtown art venues, Ferndale late-night bars. The agent understands the difference between a techno-compatible room and a bottle-service club.

- **Selector, Not Promoter**: The pitch frame is always "curator bringing a sonic journey," not "promoter who needs a Saturday." Venues fear empty rooms and logistical headaches. The agent positions the night as a turn-key audiophile experience — they provide the room, we bring the sound, the crowd, and the vibe.

- **Low-Friction CTA**: Never ask for a booking in the first email. Ask: "Are you currently booking dates for late-summer electronic slots, and who's the best person to drop a 15-minute curation mix to?" Build authentic connections, not spam signals.

- **Human-In-The-Loop**: The agent does the heavy lifting (discovery, enrichment, scoring, drafting), but a human reviews every pitch before it's sent. One bad automated rollout can burn bridges in a tight local scene.

- **Underground Authenticity**: AI-generated pitches must sound like they come from someone who lives in the culture — not a marketing intern. The Underground variant speaks as scene-member-to-scene-member. Even the Professional variant avoids corporate-speak.

## What Success Looks Like

1. **Month 1**: Agent discovers 50+ Detroit-area venues, qualifies 15-20, pitches 10-12, lands 2-3 first conversations.
2. **Month 2**: First regular psytrance night established at a Detroit venue. Agent handles follow-ups and reply negotiation.
3. **Month 3**: Expand to Midwest tour circuit (Chicago, Cleveland, Columbus, Toronto). Agent plans tour routes and coordinates multi-venue outreach.
4. **Ongoing**: Agent becomes the operational backbone — discovering new venues, tracking relationships, optimizing pitch variants, and building a Detroit psytrance scene one venue at a time.

## Architecture Principles

- **Automated Discovery**: Multi-source web scraping with proxy rotation and neighborhood-aware queries.
- **AI-Powered Qualification**: LLM + computer vision for culture-fit scoring grounded in Detroit scene knowledge.
- **Personalized Outreach**: Bespoke pitches reflecting venue-specific traits, with A/B variant optimization.
- **Predictive Intelligence**: Success probability scoring from vibe scores, city history, and trait alignment.
- **Geographic Intelligence**: Interactive mapping, proximity clustering, and AI-driven tour routing.
- **Resilient DevOps**: Autonomous repository sync, AI merge conflict resolution, tiered deployment.

## The Bigger Picture

This isn't just about booking gigs. It's about building infrastructure for a scene. Every venue that says yes becomes a node. Every successful night creates demand for the next. The agent operationalizes the tedious work of scene-building so the artist can focus on what matters: the music.
